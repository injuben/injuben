What is Fountain?

Fountain is a simple markup syntax for writing, editing and sharing screenplays in plain, human-readable text. Learn more about Fountain.

Where is the screenplay stored?

The content of the screenplay is stored in the browser's localStorage to ensure you can continue writing from your last session whenever you start the same browser. The content of your screenplay and final generated PDF file will NOT be left on the server side. Learn more about localStorage.

What operating systems and browsers are supported?

The most popular operating systems and browsers for PCs and mobile devices are supported. Chrome on desktop is recommended for the best experience.

What is the difference between the Chinese version and the English version?

The two versions have customized fonts settings and typesetting rules. For example, Source Han Serif and Source Han Sans are used in Chinese version but Courier is the only font applied to English version.

Shortcuts?

Command+S (macOS) or Ctrl+S (Windows) will refresh preview on the right PDF preview panel.
